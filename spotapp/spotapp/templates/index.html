Learn about our model

Discover new songs in a new way. We randomly sampled 10,000 spotify playlists made before the year 2017. Our approach was to use a common unsupervised learning technique - the autoencoder. Each playlist was converted to a vector and used to train the model on relationships between songs present. We then treat the unfinished playlists as a noisy vector and predict weighted representations of the likelihood of each particular song occurring.
